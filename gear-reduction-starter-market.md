<p><p>The gear reduction starter market primarily features two types: the offset gear reduction starter and the planetary gear reduction starter. Offset gear reduction starters utilize an offset drive arrangement to enhance torque while minimizing size and weight, suitable for various applications. In contrast, planetary gear reduction starters employ a system of gears arranged in a planetary configuration, offering efficient torque multiplication and compact design, making them ideal for high-performance environments. Both types serve to improve engine starting efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
