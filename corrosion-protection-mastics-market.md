<p><strong>The Corrosion Protection Mastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rubberized Bitumen Based</li><li>Petrolatum Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrosion protection mastics primarily fall into two categories: rubberized bitumen-based and petrolatum-based products. Rubberized bitumen-based mastics offer excellent adhesion and water resistance, making them suitable for various surfaces and environments, particularly in construction and marine applications. Petrolatum-based mastics, on the other hand, provide effective moisture barrier properties and are often used in pipelines and underground structures. Both types enhance durability and extend the lifespan of structures by preventing corrosion, but they cater to different application needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
