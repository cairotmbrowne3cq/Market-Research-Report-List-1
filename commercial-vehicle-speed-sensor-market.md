<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle speed sensor market comprises two main types: Hall Effect and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect speed through electronic signals, providing high accuracy and reliability. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors, often based on electromagnetic induction, generate voltage in response to wheel rotation, making them simpler and cost-effective. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring vehicle safety and performance, enabling timely speed monitoring and enhancing overall driving experience in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
