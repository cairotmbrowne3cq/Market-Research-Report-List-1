<p><strong>The Tape for Semiconductor Process Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Type</li><li>Non-UV Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the semiconductor process market, tapes are classified into UV and non-UV types based on their adhesive properties and curing methods. UV type tapes utilize ultraviolet light to cure the adhesive, offering precise control and fast processing, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal thermal exposure. In contrast, non-UV type tapes use heat or chemical reactions for curing, providing durability and strength for various packaging and interconnect processes. Both types cater to specific needs in semiconductor fabrication and assembly.</p></p>
