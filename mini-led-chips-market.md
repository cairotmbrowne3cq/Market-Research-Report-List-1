<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip LED</li><li>Vertical Chip LED</li><li>Flip Chip LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mini-LED chips market features three main types: Lateral Chip LED, Vertical Chip LED, and Flip Chip LED. Lateral Chip LEDs have a side-emitting design, ideal for enhancing color and brightness in displays. Vertical Chip LEDs are stacked vertically, offering better thermal management and higher efficiency. Flip Chip LEDs are mounted directly onto substrates, enabling improved electrical performance and reduced size. Together, these technologies contribute to advancements in display quality, energy efficiency, and compact design in various applications.</p></p>
