<p><ul><li>Asymmetrical Structure Film</li><li>Symmetrical Structure Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coextruded multi-layer film market is categorized into two main types: asymmetrical structure film and symmetrical structure film. Asymmetrical structure films consist of varying layer thicknesses, designed for specific performance attributes such as enhanced barrier properties or sealing strength. In contrast, symmetrical structure films have uniform layer thicknesses, providing balanced performance across characteristics like strength and flexibility. These film types cater to diverse packaging needs in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods, enhancing product preservation and shelf life.</p></p>
